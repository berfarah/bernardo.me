Led teams of up to 25 engineers, and 2 EMs, across 4 teams across Platform UX, Admin Experience, Maps, Billing & Operations. Grew Samsara's customer-facing platform team during the hypergrowth phase (R&D size: 70→300), supported key areas of geographic growth, and transformed Samsara's posture towards page load performance.

- **Built and scaled Platform team supporting hypergrowth:** Scaled from 3 to 25 engineers + 2 EMs in 1.5 years
- **Expanded geography to support customer growth:** Started first international engineering office. Hired first 12 engineers and EM in 6 months in London. Delivered features necessary to enter EU market, resulting in 70% decrease in lost sales due to missing features. Developed hiring plan, funnel targets, onboarding strategy for a new Poland office growing to 25 engineers in a year.
- **Led org-wide page load performance initiative:** Decreased average site load time by 60% via a variety of targeted improvements (caching, pagination, internal SLA definition and enforcement). Acted as program manager and strategic planner for initiative.
- **Led highly ambiguous projects:**
  - Tasked with improving address quality for customers, established benchmarks for reverse geocoding accuracy. Shipped improvements at scale of 25K reqs/sec, achieving accuracy improvements from 75% to 90% for the US and from 25 to 75% in Europe.
  - Tasked with improving consistency of driver assignments, unified 8 distinct sources across 5 product areas into a centralized queryable data store/service.
- **Supported scaling eng org:** Worked with engineering leaders to make improvements to interview evaluation and career ladder. Developed management and cross-team collaboration best practices that are actively used within engineering. Conducted hundreds of interviews for engineers, EMs and PMs.
